I'm Mirko, a software engineer of fifteen years, most of them spent in regulated banking and payments, where "move fast and break things" was never on the table. Today I lead AI at [Moberg](https://moberg.hr), working out how teams ship faster with AI without loosening the discipline that regulated software demands.

I care about correctness, unglamorous reliability, and tooling that makes good engineering the path of least resistance. Also, occasionally, about fixing all the things.

Open-source tooling I lead at Moberg, bringing our engineering discipline to AI-assisted development.

#### [Warp](https://github.com/moberghr/warp)
